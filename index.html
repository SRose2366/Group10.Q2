    STARFRUITS

    Your Games, Our Playthroughs

    Audience

    Our audiences are mostly teens or younger, like us. We picked them as our audience since we're the most familiar with said audience, and our topic appeal (or could appeal) to them.

    Topic

    We decided to pick this topic because we really wanted gaming but we thought that it was kind of overused..

    Purpose

    The purpose of our game review streaming is to share experiences with video games with a larger audience while giving tips (for example, if you should buy or try the game or if it's recommended). It's also for the community to connect and discuss popular games.

    Why

    We think that our topic is helpful to those people who might have no access to playing this game or just want to know the honest rating or opinion of people about the games that they are interested in. With reacting to new or old games we are able to both spread awareness and entertainment to our viewers/audiences
